---
title: Proof of Reserve
---

We have partnered with [Chainlink](https://chain.link) to provide an on-chain oracle for transparency and auditability of RenVM's cross-chain assets bridged into the DeFi ecosystem, starting with renBTC, renBCH, and renZEC. This can be useful to de-risk or insure systems that hold Ren-tokenized assets.

You can more information about the API and the Ethereum address for the Proof of Reserve oracle [here](https://market.link/feeds/342711bf-ff2d-481b-89cd-488d33ae88de?network=1).
